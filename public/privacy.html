Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Juan's World collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. Transparency is a core principle here — we believe you should know exactly what happens to your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, and message content sent through the Ask Juan form.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data via Vercel Analytics (page views, performance metrics). No personal identifiers are collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • Understanding site traffic and improving the user experience

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your contact form data is processed through Resend for email delivery and is subject to their privacy practices. No other third parties have access to your data.

4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Analytics data is retained per Vercel's standard retention policies.

5. Cookies

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Vercel Analytics may use minimal first-party cookies for anonymous session measurement.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Ask Juan form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Significant changes will be noted here with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the Ask Juan form or the contact details provided there.
